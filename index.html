NovaQuest Staff Rules & Expectations

This form was created for staff to understand they are held to higher standards than regular players, and for the community to see that NovaQuest is fully transparent regarding staff actions and any issues that may arise. It includes updates on rules, staff demotions, and other relevant matters, while demonstrating our commitment to keeping the NovaQuest community safe, fair, and enjoyable for everyone.

General Staff Duties

  • Prioritize staff responsibilities over gameplay: respond to player questions, tickets, and reports promptly.
  • Earn or buy all items/ranks like regular players unless officially rewarded by the founder (Puchu AFK).
  • Maintain professionalism in all interactions with players and staff.
  • Follow all hierarchy rules — lower staff report to higher staff as defined in this guide.
  • Do not hint at promotions — all promotions are decided by management and the founder.
  • Do not leak confidential information (staff tickets, staff Discord, internal discussions):
    • Admin+ leaking staff info → instant demotion
    • Leaking management server info → immediate removal from network
  • Report bugs within 24 hours. Using unreported bugs for personal gain is a strikeable offense.
  • Do not close tickets prematurely; tickets may only be closed if:
    • The player hasn’t replied within 24 hours
    • The player is uncooperative as determined by management

Strike System

  • Staff receive 3 strikes, resetting every 6 months, all strikes are logged permanently.
  • Accumulation or severity of strikes may lead to demotion or removal.
  • Strikeable offenses include (but are not limited to):
    • Impolite behavior toward players or staff
    • Misuse of staff commands (trolling, unfair advantage)
    • Failure to report bugs or misuse of unreported bugs
    • Leaking confidential information

Professional Interaction Rules

  • Staff cannot ignore players or ongoing issues.
  • All player interactions must remain professional.
  • Issues escalating between staff and players must be reported to management if widespread.
  • Staff may not use admin/staff commands for personal gain, trolling, or assisting friends.
  • All actions taken must be documented if requested by management.

Hierarchy & Responsibilities

  • Helpers: Answer player questions and concerns, report bugs using YouTrack templates, Permissions: Warn, Kick, Mute
  • Junior Moderators (JrMods): Handle exploiters and ban-worthy violations, Access moderation systems, Permissions: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Access
  • Moderators (Mods): Supervise Helpers & JrMods, guide lower staff, fully understand in-game mechanics and rules
  • Senior Moderators (SrMods): Train and mentor lower staff, accountable for supervised staff knowledge, reviewed for Admin promotion
  • Admins & Higher: Handle all server issues, ensure staff follow rules, leaking confidential info → immediate demotion/removal

Permissions

  • Helpers: Warn, Kick, Mute
  • JrMods: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Access
  • Mods: All JrMod permissions + authority over Helpers & JrMods
  • SrMods: All Mod permissions + mentor lower staff
  • Admins+: All permissions + high-level issue resolution

Activity & Inactivity

  • No minimum activity required, but management monitors engagement.
  • Inactivity >3 months may result in demotion, with a possibility of reinstatement at last issued role.
  • Resignation allows potential reinstatement at last role, except for SrMods.
  • SrMods are expected to maintain high activity; inactivity may result in demotion with no guaranteed reinstatement.

Conclusion

By following this guide, staff ensure fair, transparent, and professional operations across NovaQuest. All rules are objective, enforceable, and apply equally to all staff, leaving no ambiguity or excuse.
